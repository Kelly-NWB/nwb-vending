How to print

Size: US Letter, 8.5 x 11 inches. Two pages (front + back).

At home or on a shop printer: Use the HOME PDF if we sent two files. Paper = Letter. Scale = Actual size or 100%. Do not use Fit to page or Shrink to fit. Two-sided = flip on the long edge (normal portrait). Turn borderless printing off unless you already know that setting on your printer.

Bottom cut off? That is normal on home inkjets. They cannot print edge to edge. The bottom quarter inch (sometimes more) is dead zone. The HOME PDF leaves extra room so the footer survives. If it still clips, take the file to FedEx Office, Staples, or Office Depot. Their machines print it right. Usually about two bucks.

Mass batch at FedEx? Use the full-page PDF (no HOME in the name). That is the version built for commercial print.

Still stuck? Message us. Send a photo of what came out of your printer. We will tell you which file and setting to use.
