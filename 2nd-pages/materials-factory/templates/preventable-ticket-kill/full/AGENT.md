# mf-tpl-089 · Preventable Ticket Kill Playbook

**Audience:** agents. Solve problems before they become tickets. Factory welcome packs, default passwords in the box, human phone openers with location. Design out the obvious call.

## Apply
1. Hunt → **volume-class-hunt**.
2. In-box / defaults → **welcome-pack-spec** + **factory-default-card**.
3. Voice trust → **open-line-script**.
4. Prove → **class-volume-before-after**.
5. Pair **mf-tpl-027** onboarding; **mf-tpl-065** unit cost of the class you killed.

## Use when
High volume of preventable tickets (e.g. every ship calls for password). Need welcome pack or upstream kill. Phone open that signals real location/team.
