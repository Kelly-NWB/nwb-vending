# mf-tool-003 outcomes

## yes_courtesy_once

Tiny fix once. Log it.

## fee_required

Medium change. Change order before work.

## fee_rush_or_decline

Hard deadline + extra scope. Rush fee or later date.

## fee_or_no

Thin margin. Paid add-on or original scope only.

## fee_or_trade

Friends/family. Price or trade, warm tone.

## defer_phase_two

Good add-on after v1 ships.

## requote_before_work

Large change mid-job. Pause and re-quote.

## requote_new_job

After delivery. New intake (mf-tpl-004).

## freeze_and_document

No written scope. Email in/out list, freeze work.

## no_polite

Decline with reason (manual pick when out of skill/capacity).

## escalate_complaint

Human routes to mf-tpl-001 when tone is recovery not scope.
