# mf-tpl-099 · Service Operations Desk Playbook

SOD: execution arm of post-sales. Traditional RMA/shipping/returns group so support engineers stop shipping parts and chase paperwork. Support solves; SOD closes, returns, collects, templates.

## Apply
1. Eng shipping → **sod-charter**.
2. Always **support-to-sod-handoff** before ship.
3. Dump risk → **scope-lock**.
4. Weekly **sod-scorecard**.
5. Pair **mf-tpl-101** parts recovery; **mf-tpl-088** so engineers stay engineers.
